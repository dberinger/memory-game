Memory game

Welcome to the memory game!

Here are the rules:

  • each round you have two clicks to reveal underlying symbols of two chosen cards
  • if two symbols match, the two cards stay flipped over, so remember the symbols you've discovered before
  • if cards don't match, they remain face down
  • in order to win, all cards must be matched
  • there is no time or click limit
