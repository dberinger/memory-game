The memory game

Here are the rules:

  • each round you have two clicks to reveal underlying symbols of two chosen cards
  • if two symbols match, the two cards stay flipped over
  • if cards don't match, they'll flip facing down
  • in order to win, all cards must be matched
  • there is no time or click limit
